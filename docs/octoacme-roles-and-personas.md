# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile processes and remove impediments that prevent the team from delivering value. They coach teams on agile practices, protect them from external interruptions, and foster continuous improvement through retrospectives and process refinement.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate sprint planning, daily standups, reviews, and retrospectives
- Identify and help remove blockers and impediments
- Coach team members and stakeholders on agile practices
- Maintain sprint board and ensure process transparency
- Escalate issues that cannot be resolved within the team
- Drive retrospective action items and process improvements

### Goals
- Enable team autonomy and self-organization
- Maximize team velocity and sustainable pace
- Foster psychological safety and continuous learning
- Remove barriers to efficient delivery

### Key Interactions
- **Developers:** Daily support, blocker removal, process coaching
- **Project Manager:** Risk escalation, sprint planning, timeline coordination
- **Product Manager:** Backlog grooming, acceptance criteria clarity, sprint forecasting

### Typical Communication
- Standup facilitation and impediment tracking
- Retrospective notes and action item follow-up
- Process improvement proposals to stakeholders

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers own the user experience strategy, conduct research, and create designs that ensure products are usable, accessible, and delightful. They advocate for users throughout the product lifecycle and collaborate closely with product and engineering teams.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research, interviews, and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Define interaction patterns and design systems
- Ensure designs meet accessibility standards (WCAG compliance)
- Validate designs with users and iterate based on feedback
- Collaborate with developers to ensure design fidelity in implementation

### Goals
- Deliver user-centered, intuitive experiences
- Reduce support burden through better UX
- Increase user satisfaction and retention
- Build a cohesive, scalable design system

### Key Interactions
- **Product Manager:** User research insights, requirements, success metrics
- **Developers:** Design feasibility, implementation guidance, design system maintenance
- **QA/Testing:** Acceptance criteria for usability, accessibility validation

### Typical Communication
- Design mockups and prototypes in Figma or similar tools
- Research findings and user insights
- Design reviews and accessibility reports

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain the infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and deployment automation that enable reliable, secure, and scalable delivery. They bridge development and operations to ensure smooth deployments and system reliability.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines and automated testing infrastructure
- Manage cloud environments, containerization, and orchestration
- Implement and monitor deployment strategies (blue-green, canary, rolling updates)
- Ensure security scanning and compliance in deployment pipelines
- Monitor system performance, uptime, and incident response procedures
- Document runbooks and disaster recovery procedures

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable, and repeatable deployments
- Reduce deployment risk and time-to-recovery
- Maintain high system availability and security posture
- Automate repetitive operational tasks

### Key Interactions
- **Developers:** Pipeline feedback, build optimization, deployment guidance
- **QA/Testing:** Test environment provisioning, staging deployment support
- **Project Manager:** Deployment scheduling, rollback procedures

### Typical Communication
- Deployment pipeline documentation and runbooks
- Infrastructure-as-Code repositories
- Incident response and post-mortems

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts transform raw data into actionable insights that inform product and business decisions. They design dashboards, monitor key metrics, and provide evidence-based recommendations to drive prioritization and strategy.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain dashboards for key performance indicators (KPIs)
- Conduct data analysis and generate insights from user behavior and system metrics
- Support A/B testing and experimentation frameworks
- Create reports for stakeholder and executive communication
- Monitor product health metrics, errors, and performance
- Identify data quality issues and recommend data governance improvements

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision-making at all levels
- Quantify impact of features and improvements
- Identify trends and opportunities for optimization
- Support continuous improvement with evidence

### Key Interactions
- **Product Manager:** KPI definition, prioritization insights, experiment design
- **Project Manager:** Metrics tracking, success measurement, project retrospectives
- **Developers:** Instrumentation guidance, query optimization, data availability

### Typical Communication
- Dashboard updates and metric reports
- Analysis findings and recommendations
- Data documentation and metadata

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge the gap between business stakeholders and the delivery team by gathering requirements, understanding processes, and ensuring solutions align with business needs and constraints. They translate business language into actionable specifications.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document business requirements and acceptance criteria
- Model business processes and identify improvement opportunities
- Create specifications and user stories from requirements
- Validate solutions against business objectives
- Coordinate with subject matter experts (SMEs) and stakeholders
- Manage scope and communicate change impacts

### Goals
- Ensure solutions solve real business problems
- Reduce rework due to misaligned requirements
- Improve communication between business and technical teams
- Enable faster time-to-value

### Key Interactions
- **Product Manager:** Requirements refinement, business case validation, stakeholder alignment
- **Developers:** Specification clarity, edge case identification, implementation feasibility
- **Project Manager:** Scope management, stakeholder communication, change tracking

### Typical Communication
- Requirements documentation and user story creation
- Process flow diagrams and business process documentation
- Stakeholder meeting facilitation and change impact assessments
